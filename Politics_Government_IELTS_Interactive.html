üîë Key Vocabulary

Democracy
A system where people choose their leaders by voting
Dictatorship
A system where one person or a small group has all the power
Censorship
Control of information by the government, preventing people from seeing or publishing certain ideas
Freedom of speech
The right to say what you think, even if others disagree
Human rights
Universal rights (life, freedom, equality) that every person should have
Political corruption
When politicians use power unfairly for personal gain
Propaganda
Information (often biased or misleading) spread to influence people's opinions
Civil rights
Basic rights and freedoms (e.g., voting, equality before the law)
Transparency
Openness and honesty in government actions
Constitution
The main set of rules/laws that defines how a country is governed
