üìö PASSIVE VOICE ‚Äî Quick Reference

Structure: be + past participle (V3)

Present Simple: is/are + V3
‚Üí "The company is led by a woman"
Past Simple: was/were + V3
‚Üí "She was appointed CEO in 2008"
Present Perfect: has/have been + V3
‚Üí "Awards have been given to her"
Future: will be + V3
‚Üí "New projects will be launched next year"
